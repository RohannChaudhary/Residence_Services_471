Univerity of Calgary

About

A public research university in Calgary, Alberta.

First Year

All first year students under the age of 21 live in single or double occupancy rooms in a first year dormitory-style residence and are guaranteed residence for the first two years of their study at the University of Calgary.

Learn More

Second Year

All second year students, including transfer and international students, live in in furnished two or three bedroom suite-style apartments with a kitchenette, bathroom, and a private bedroom.

Learn More

Upper Year

Undergraduate students in their third year or higher of post-secondary and under the age of 27 have the option of living in furnished studio, one bedroom, two bedroom, and four bedroom units with full kitchens.

Learn More

Graduate

Graduate students, students that have completed an undergraduate degree or mature students (27+ years old) have the option of living in one bedroom, studio, or two bedroom apartments in Crowsnest Hall.

Learn More

Family Housing

Residents must reside with a spouse or common-law partner (with or without children), or as a single parent living with children. Applicants must be affiliated with the university.

Learn More
